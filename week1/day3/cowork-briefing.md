Cowork — Quick Briefing
What it's for: Recurring, rule-based tasks that eat up time but don't require judgment — reports, alerts, drafts, file organization.
What it can automate: Weekly sales summaries, low-inventory alerts, order confirmation drafts, scheduled emails, spreadsheet updates.
What still needs a human eye: Anything going to a customer, financial decisions, anything new or outside the normal routine.
One workflow to build next: A weekly low-inventory alert — Cowork checks Shopify stock levels every Monday and emails a reorder list for anything below your threshold.
