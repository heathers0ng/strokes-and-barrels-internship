Shopify Sales Report Generator is a Python script that connects to the Shopify Admin REST API to pull all orders from the last 30 days and produce a per-product sales breakdown. It handles pagination automatically to retrieve the complete order history, filters out cancelled orders, and aggregates units sold and net revenue, accounting for line-level discounts, for every product sold. Results are exported to sales_report.csv, sorted by revenue from highest to lowest, giving an at-a-glance view of top and underperforming products.
